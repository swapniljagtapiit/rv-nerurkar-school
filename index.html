About Our School

Established in 1978, R.V. Nerurkar High School and Junior College has grown from a humble initiative into a cornerstone of education in Dombivli's Anand Nagar area. Over four decades, we have expanded to serve thousands of local students with a commitment to holistic development.

Our vision is to create responsible, socially conscious citizens by providing quality education that balances academic rigor with moral and physical development. We offer an inclusive learning environment for students from all backgrounds.

  • Affiliated with Maharashtra State Board
  • 40+ Years of Educational Excellence
  • Marathi Medium & Junior College

Our Academic Programs

A comprehensive Marathi-medium curriculum and multi-stream Junior College.

Secondary (Std 5–10)

Robust SSC Maharashtra State Board curriculum focusing on core subjects: Mathematics, Science, and Social Sciences.

Junior College (Std 11–12)

Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) courses with specialized streams in Science, Commerce, and Arts.

Special Focus

Dedicated programs for Computer Education, Physical Education, and Languages (Marathi, English, Hindi/Sanskrit).
